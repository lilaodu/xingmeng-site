Internal Workflow

The project is designed for internal reporting, dashboard analysis, and human-reviewed optimization decisions for our own Amazon Ads account.

  • Amazon Ads campaign reporting and account-level performance review for our own account
  • Sponsored Products, Sponsored Brands, and Sponsored Display performance analysis
  • Keyword, target, and search term performance organization
  • Budget and bid monitoring for internal advertising operations
  • Optimization recommendations based on internal rules and human review

Data Use

Xingmeng only uses authorized Amazon Ads data from our own advertising account for internal application functions, such as reporting, analysis, troubleshooting, and account management. We do not sell advertising data.
